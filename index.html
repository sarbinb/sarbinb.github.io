Getting Here

Flights Accomodations

San Francisco

  • 45 minutes
  • RentalUberTaxis
  • SFO is usually the cheapest and easiest to find flights. It's a straightforward drive down US 101 or the scenic I-280. Rush hours are M-F 4PM to 7:30PM & 6:30AM to 10AM. Rush hour travel can double travel time. We'd recommend I-280 during rush hour--at least you get to be stuck looking at pretty scenary.

San Jose

  • 20 minutes
  • RentalTaxis
  • San Jose is the most convient airport, sometimes has cheaper flights, but flights usually have at least 1 layover.

Oakland

  • 65 minutes
  • RentalUberTaxis
  • Oakland is the third Bay Area airport. Your drive will take you down I-880. Rush hour is M-F 4PM to 7:30PM & 6:30AM to 10AM. Rush hour traffic can double travel time.

Airbnb

  • $$$$
  • 10-30 mins
  • Our top recommendation for out-of-town guests. You get the cozy feeling of home at better prices than hotels. If you're like us, pick a home with fun decorations.
  • Book near Saratoga, Cupertino or Los Gatos.
  • Choose a private room to make local friends or choose entire home for privacy.
